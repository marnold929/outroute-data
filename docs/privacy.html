OutRoute Privacy Policy

Effective July 17, 2026

OutRoute is built to know a lot about fantasy football and nothing about you.

What we collect

Nothing. OutRoute has no accounts, no sign-up, no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. The one optional exception is The Analyst chat feature, described below — and nothing there happens unless you use it.

What stays on your device

Your league settings, roster, draft board, waiver list, and (if you use league sync) your league ID and any ESPN session cookies you enter are stored only on your device, in the app's private storage. They are never sent to us — we have no servers that receive user data.

Network requests the app makes

  • Rankings feed: the app downloads a public player-rankings file (a static JSON document). This request contains no personal information.
  • Sleeper API: if you use Sleeper league sync or trending data, the app calls Sleeper's public API directly from your device using the username or league you specify.
  • ESPN league sync: if you connect an ESPN league, the app calls ESPN's league API directly from your device, using the league ID (and, for private leagues, the session cookies) you provide. These credentials go only from your device to ESPN and are stored only on your device.

AI Analyst (optional feature)

The Analyst is an optional in-app chat. If — and only if — you use it:

  • What is sent: the chat messages you type and a summary of your league context (league settings, roster, and waiver list) are sent to our server and relayed to an AI provider (Anthropic) to generate the reply. We do not store your messages or league context; they are processed to produce the response.
  • Device ID: an anonymous, app-generated device identifier is used to track your Analyst credit balance. It is random, created by the app, and not tied to your name, Apple ID, or any account.
  • Purchases: Analyst credit purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details.
  • If you never open The Analyst, none of the above happens — no messages, no league summary, and no device ID ever leave your phone.

Children

OutRoute does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.
