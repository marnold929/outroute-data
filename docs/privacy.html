OutRoute Privacy Policy

Effective July 15, 2026

OutRoute is built to know a lot about fantasy football and nothing about you.

What we collect

Nothing. OutRoute has no accounts, no sign-up, no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

What stays on your device

Your league settings, roster, draft board, waiver list, and (if you use league sync) your league ID and any ESPN session cookies you enter are stored only on your device, in the app's private storage. They are never sent to us — we have no servers that receive user data.

Network requests the app makes

  • Rankings feed: the app downloads a public player-rankings file (a static JSON document). This request contains no personal information.
  • Sleeper API: if you use Sleeper league sync or trending data, the app calls Sleeper's public API directly from your device using the username or league you specify.
  • ESPN league sync: if you connect an ESPN league, the app calls ESPN's league API directly from your device, using the league ID (and, for private leagues, the session cookies) you provide. These credentials go only from your device to ESPN and are stored only on your device.

Children

OutRoute does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
